Ingredients

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Cinnamon Buns To Die For

Read time: 5 minutes

Baking cinnamon buns is more than just creating a delicious treat—it’s a rewarding experience that fills your home with warmth and comfort. The process of kneading dough, watching it rise, and filling it with cinnamon sugar creates a sense of accomplishment, while the sweet, spicy aroma of baking buns instantly lifts the mood. Sharing fresh, homemade cinnamon buns with family or friends brings joy and connection, turning any moment into a special occasion. Plus, homemade buns are far superior to store-bought ones, with their soft, gooey centers and rich flavor. It’s the perfect way to indulge!

Warm, soft, and irresistibly sweet, cinnamon buns are a classic treat perfect for breakfast or dessert. These swirled delights, filled with a rich cinnamon-sugar mixture and topped with a creamy glaze, offer the perfect balance of spice and sweetness. Whether you're a baking enthusiast or a beginner, this recipe will guide you through creating soft, gooey cinnamon buns that will fill your home with the comforting aroma of cinnamon and make any moment feel extra special.

This cinnamon bun recipe stands out for its simplicity and unbeatable flavor. It’s designed to give you soft, fluffy rolls with just the right amount of cinnamon sweetness, perfect for beginners and seasoned bakers alike. The dough is easy to work with, and the recipe includes tips for a foolproof rise, ensuring each bun turns out perfectly light and tender. Plus, the rich, gooey filling and homemade glaze add a bakery-quality touch that will make these buns a family favorite. If you're looking for a reliable, crowd-pleasing recipe, this one’s a must-try!

Recipe

Make the dough: In a large bowl, combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Add warm milk, melted butter, and egg, mixing until a soft dough forms. Knead for about 5 minutes, then cover and let rise for 1 hour.

Prepare the filling: Mix together softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a small bowl.

Shape the buns: Roll out the dough into a rectangle (about 12x9 inches). Spread the filling evenly over the dough. Roll the dough up tightly, then slice it into 9-12 equal rolls.

Second rise: Place the rolls in a greased baking dish. Cover and let rise for another 30 minutes.

Bake: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the rolls for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.

Glaze: Mix powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth. Drizzle over warm cinnamon buns.

Enjoy!
